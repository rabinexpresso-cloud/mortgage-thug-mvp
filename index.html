📖 How to Play
कसरी खेल्ने?
1
Add all players in clockwise order. Minimum 3 players required.
2
Choose one of 6 mortgage categories. A secret word is randomly picked from it.
3
Pass the phone to each player. Drag the pink card to see your role. One player will be the धितो ठग (Mortgage Thug) — they don't see the word.
4
Go around the circle. Each player says one vague word clue about the secret word. The ठग tries to guess it from the clues.
5
Discuss & vote for who you think is the ठग. Majority vote decides.
6
If caught, the ठग gets one chance to guess the secret word. Guess correctly = ठग wins!
7
If the ठग is NOT caught, the ठग wins. Otherwise, the team wins!
